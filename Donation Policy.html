Donation Policy

We at "Sihatkum" appreciate your support and donation of medical equipment to support our efforts in improving healthcare and providing medical services to those who need them most. Please read our medical equipment donation policy below:

1. Type of equipment accepted:

We accept donations of medical equipment that is in good condition and usable, such as pressure and sugar devices, medical and electrical chairs, and medical devices used in treatment and diagnosis.

2. Donation Process:

To donate medical equipment, click the "Donation" button in the navigation bar at the top of the page. You will be directed to the donation page where you can fill out the necessary donation form. Enter the product name, its condition, and any additional notes you would like to provide. Upload a picture of the product to facilitate its evaluation. After completing the form, press the "Submit" button to send the donation information. Our team will review your request to ensure compliance with acceptance requirements. Through this process, you can easily and conveniently donate medical equipment, effectively contributing to our efforts to improve healthcare.

3. Rejection of Donations:

We reserve the right to refuse any donation that is deemed inappropriate or does not comply with the terms of donation.

4. Privacy Policy:

We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of donor information and do not share this information with any external party without explicit consent from the donors.

5. Donation Inquiries:

For any inquiries or questions regarding the donation process, please contact us through the available means on the website.
